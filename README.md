# MediCard

**MediCard** is an all-in-one health app that provides quick access to critical medical information during emergencies. With a credit card-sized NFC-enabled PCB, users can tap the card against a phone to instantly send a distress signal to emergency contacts, sharing location and vital medical details. The app also features an AI-powered allergy scanner, a comprehensive first aid guide with AR integration, and tools for managing family profiles and medication reminders.
